Symptom Tracker UK

In the UK, we're all worried about the coronavirus. Unfortunately, due to a lack of of test kits, we don't know exactly how many people have been infected. In this mini-survey, we're trying to ask people all around the UK about any coronavirus-related symptoms they've experienced in the last four weeks, so we can have a better idea of the spread of the disease.

Importantly, this doesn't work if only sick people complete the survey. We need everyone, including those who are currently sick, people who have been sick recently, and people who don't feel sick at all.

By filling out this survey, you can help us understand the spread of this disease, and do your bit to help stop it. It should take about 2 minutes. You can complete this survey as many times you like, as your symptoms change, but please enter your email address so we know the data is coming from the same person.

Who we are

This is an unofficial, volunteer-run project, and is not currently supported by the government, any official body, or a university.

What happens to your data?

Most of the data you provide will be made public, as soon as possible. This is so that researchers around the world can analyse it and put it to use.

Disclaimer

By completing this survey, you consent for us to store and access the data you provide, and to make the non-personal data (everything except your email address, IP address, and full postcode) publicly available.
