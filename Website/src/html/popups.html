Who we are

This is an unofficial, volunteer-run project, and is not currently supported by the government, any official body, or a university. It is led by Dr Eoin Travers, a psychologist at University College London. However, this is a personal project, and is not associated with University College London in any way.

If you have any questions or comments, or would like to volunteer to help with this project, please email symptom.tracker.uk@gmail.com. You can find more details about the kind of help that might be needed in this post.

What happens to your data

Most of the data you provide will be made public, as soon as possible. This is so that researchers around the world can analyse it and put it to use.

However, some of the questions ask for personal information, such as your email address or full postcode. We won't share this data with anyone else without your permission.
